How to Make Money on Instagram ? - English

vijay kumar
  • March 11, 2022

Ways to Make Money on Instagram in English:

We all know how famous Instagram is these days. What started as a photo sharing app has now become one of the best social media apps. However, many have only shared their photos on Instagram. But some people are making millions through this.

So we also need to have as many followers as we need to make money through Instagram. But here we have a doubt. Celebrities have a lot of followers but ordinary people like us have a lot of followers. But you do not have to be a celebrity to get followers on Instagram.

How To Become Famous On Instagram?

Followers will automatically come if you take any category you are interested in and create an Instagram page related to it and post good posts related to that category every day. If not it will take time. In the next posts we will learn about the topic of how to get more followers to your Instagram page.

Now let’s see how to make money through Instagram. There are three ways to earn money on Instagram.

  • Becoming an Influencer
  • Doing Affiliate Marketing
  • Selling our own products
    •

Let us learn about each of these in detail

1.Becoming an Influencer:

This is the way most people make money on Instagram. When our page has a large number of followers, some companies in the field of which category our page belongs to will contact us and ask us to put a post about their brand on our page. They will give us some money for what we post. How Much Money Is Given How Many Followers Does Our Page Have ?, How Much Engagement Is There When We Post That means how many likes, comments and shares are coming to the post? All these are taken into consideration and the amount is given accordingly. Many celebrities around the world charge a few crores of rupees for uploading a brand related post on their Instagram page.

We too can earn at least thousands if not millions of followers for our Instagram page. All we need to do is give them an outlet and the support they need to keep going.

2.Doing Affiliate Marketing:

Affiliate is a great way for those who want to make money online. Let's see how to make money through affiliate using Instagram page.

For example, suppose you have an Instagram page about a technology that has thousands of followers. You need to upload a post about a recently released phone, laptop or any other gadget and give an affiliate link to that product in your Instagram page bio. Now if someone clicks on that link and gets that product naughty we get 3 to 5% of that product cost. The more people naughty those products, the more money you get. This is called affiliate marketing. For this you need to join affiliate on sites like Amazon, Flipkart.

3.Selling our own products:

We promote other people's product in two ways mentioned above. But in this way we promote our own product. We have to have our own product for that. Many people write and sell an e-book, or create and sell a course, or promote their website or their services. For example suppose a person has good expertise in the stock market. He creates a course about the stock market and promotes it through his Instagram page. In this way we can also earn money by promoting our own product.

You can also make money on Instagram through Shoutouts. That means another page will ask them to promote their page on our page. These are called Shoutouts. Pay a certain amount for doing Shoutouts like that.

You can also earn money by uploading videos via IGTV on Instagram. Like YouTube, Instagram also places ads in the middle of the video and gives the creator money.

This way we can make money through Instagram in many ways. But Money also comes automatically if we try to gain more followers rather than on money before. So whatever category you are interested in be it Technology, Health, Business, Education, Cooking, select any one category, create an Instagram page and increase followers by posting regularly.
