What is CIBIL Score ?

vijay kumar
  • February 27, 2022

What is CIBIL Score or Credit Score:

We resort to banks when we need a loan in an emergency. At that time the bank will first check our CIBIL Score or Credit Score. If our CIBIL score is good, we will be given a loan. Otherwise the loan may not be given. Almost any bank will give a loan based on this CIBIL score. So there is something we all need to know about this Sibyl score. So let us know about this Credit Score in detail.

What is CIBIL Score?

The CIBIL Score is a three-digit number issued by the Credit Information Bureau India Limited (CIBIL). It ranges from 300 to 900. Loans to an individual take into account the details of their payments and give a sibyl score based on them. The CIBIL department has all the details of their loans and their payments. How Much Money Does a Person Take? Are you repaying the loan on time? A score is given to him after considering all the transactions in his credit card. It ranges from 300 to 900. The closer our CIBIL score is to 900, the better it is. If the score is over 750, it means that the person's credit score is good.

What is the use of having a high credit score?

If you go to the bank for a loan, they will check your CIBIL score. The higher your CIBIL score, the more likely you are to get a loan quickly. Having a low CIBIL score does not mean that you are in debt. A loan can be given by one bank but not by another bank. It depends on the policy of the respective banks. If any bank gives him a loan with a higher interest rate (Interest Rate). That means he has to pay more interest.

Excellent Score (750 – 900): If your CIBIL score is between 750 and 900 you can easily get any loan. That too can be obtained with a low interest rate.

Good Score (700-749):If your score is between 700 and 749 you will get no loan. But you will have to pay more interest.

Fair (650-699):Secured loans are available if your score is between 650 and 699. That means you can get a loan to take out something like a car or a house. But can not get an unsecured loan. This means that you can not get loans for personal needs, education loans, credit card.

Low (below 550): If your score is less than 550 then banks are less inclined to give you a loan. Getting a loan from any of your banks can be very difficult.

How to know your CIBIL Score?

If you want to know your credit score or Sibyl score you will need to apply for a Sibyl score. For that you go to the official website of CIBIL : www.cibil.com

  1. First you need to complete the form online.
  2. You need to complete your PAN card and bank details.
  3. If you want to know your Sibyl score you have to pay some fee.
  4. The credit report will then be sent to you via this mail.

However, on some websites we can also find out the CIBIL Score for free.

  1. bankbazaar.com
  2. creditsudhaar.com
  3. freescoreindia.com

If you log in to these websites and enter your details, your CIBIL score details will be sent to your mail.

Factors Affecting CIBIL Score:

  1. If we take a loan from banks and delay their payments the score will go down. So it is always a good idea to pay off bank loans or credit card bills by the due date.
  2. Unsecured loans means that the higher the amount of loans taken by an individual for past needs, the lower the credit score.
  3. Secured loans means that the credit score will increase if you take out loans for houses and vehicles.
  4. Also, taking more loans in less time will reduce the score.

So from now on also consider credit score or CIBIL score. Increase the CIBIL score and keep that score above 750.
